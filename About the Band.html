Linkin Park is an American rock band made in 1996 from Agoura Hills, California. When in high school, three members, Mike Shinoda, Rob Bourdon, and Brad Delson. After they graduated high school, they took the music business more seriously that's when they recruited Joe Hahn, Dave "Phoenix" Farrell, and Mark Wakefield to perform in their band.
